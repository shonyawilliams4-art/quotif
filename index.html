Questions, Answered

Frequently asked

What exactly is the $99 Business Overview Audit™?

It's a flat-fee, whole-business diagnostic. We review your entity setup, books, compliance posture, credit profile and operations, then deliver a written report with a scored foundation assessment and a 90-day priority roadmap — reviewed with you 1:1.

Why does a former IRS Revenue Agent's perspective matter?

Shonya Williams worked in the IRS Small Business/Self-Employed Division, examining exactly the kinds of businesses we now serve. That means your foundation is built to the standard that matters — the one examiners and lenders actually use.

Do I need the audit before other services?

It's the smartest first step for most owners — it tells us (and you) precisely what to fix first. But if you already know your gap, you can start directly with any service, from accounting setup to funding readiness.
