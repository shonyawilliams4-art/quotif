Industries We Serve

Every industry has its own fault lines.

From trust accounting in law firms to job costing in construction, we speak your industry's financial language — and know where its foundations usually crack.

Professional Services

Consultants, agencies and advisors: productize your pricing, clean up project profitability, and build the systems that let you sell expertise instead of hours.

Law Firms

Trust-account (IOLTA) compliance, matter-level profitability, partner compensation clarity and SOPs that keep the practice — and the bar — happy.

Real Estate

Investors, brokers and property managers: entity structuring, portfolio-level books, 1099 workflows and funding readiness for the next acquisition.

Healthcare

Private practices and clinics: revenue-cycle clarity, payer-mix reporting, compliant books and SOPs that survive staff turnover.

Construction

Job costing that tells the truth, progress billing, retainage tracking, bonding-ready financials and the credit profile to win bigger contracts.

Restaurants

Prime-cost dashboards, daily sales reconciliation, tip compliance and cash controls — margins are thin; your visibility shouldn't be.

Nonprofits

Fund accounting, grant tracking and board-ready reporting that protects your status and earns funder confidence, year after year.

Government Contractors

DCAA-aware accounting, indirect-rate structure, timekeeping SOPs and audit-ready compliance — built by someone who audited for the government.

Retail

Inventory-true books, sales-tax compliance across channels, margin analytics and the funding readiness to stock up ahead of your busy season.

Service Businesses

Trades, cleaning, landscaping, salons and beyond: pricing that protects profit, dispatch-to-invoice workflows and books that make you bankable.
