Quoti Live FAQ

Fair questions

Is Quoti Live really free? What's the catch?

Really free — all 10,000 quotes, search, favorites, sharing and image downloads. No account, no trial, no locked tier. If it helps you, an optional $1 (or custom) donation keeps it running. That's the entire model.

Where do the quotes come from?

Every quote is an original aphorism written for the Quoti Live collection — no recycled internet lists, no misattributed "Einstein" quotes. Twenty categories, exactly 500 quotes each.

Can I use the quotes in my content?

Yes — share them, post them, print them. A credit to Quoti Live is appreciated but not required. The download button even makes you a ready-to-post image card.

Where are my favorites stored?

Privately, in your own browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent to a server, which is also why no account is needed.
